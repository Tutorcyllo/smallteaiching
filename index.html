Bridging Medical Schools:
Western Clinical Immersion in Spain
for Traditional Chinese Medicine Students

医学院交流项目：中医学生西班牙西方临床沉浸课程

A four-week intensive summer programme — July 2026 · Valladolid, Spain

四周强化暑期课程 — 2026年7月 · 西班牙巴利亚多利德

🗓 July 2026 · 2026年7月 📍 Valladolid, Spain · 西班牙巴利亚多利德 ⏱ 4 Weeks · 4周 🎓 Medicine & TCM · 医学与中医

This programme offers TCM students from Zhejiang University School of Medicine an immersive encounter with Western biomedical education and clinical practice, hosted by the Faculty of Medicine of the University of Valladolid. Over four structured weeks, participants will explore anatomy, physiology, and pharmacology; learn the Western clinical method enriched by AI tools; shadow hospital teams in real clinical settings; and engage in a meaningful dialogue between Traditional Chinese Medicine and Western Medicine.

本项目由巴利亚多利德大学医学院承办，为浙江大学医学院中医专业学生提供与西方生物医学教育及临床实践深度融合的沉浸式体验。在四周结构化课程中，学员将探索解剖学、生理学与药理学，学习融合人工智能工具的西方临床方法，在真实临床环境中跟随医院团队实习，并开展传统中医与西方医学之间富有意义的对话。

Programme Structure 课程结构

Block I · Week 1 · 第一模块·第一周
Biomedical Foundations Bootcamp
生物医学基础强化营
Morning theory + morning lab practicals · 上午理论课 + 上午实验实践

Morning theory sessions provide a "big-picture" overview of each discipline — its core concepts, current role in modern medicine, and major evolutionary directions — followed by morning lab-style practicals connecting mechanisms with real clinical examples.

上午理论课以宏观视角介绍每门学科的核心概念、在现代医学中的地位及主要发展方向，随后进行实验式实践课，将理论机制与真实临床案例相结合。

  • Day 1: Anatomy — structure & clinical relevance第一天：解剖学 — 结构与临床意义
  • Day 2: Physiology — core regulatory systems第二天：生理学 — 核心调节系统
  • Day 3: Cell Biology & Immunology第三天：细胞生物学与免疫学
  • Day 4: Microbiology — pathogens & host defence第四天：微生物学 — 病原体与宿主防御
  • Day 5: Pharmacology — drug mechanisms & safety第五天：药理学 — 药物机制与安全性
Block II · Week 2 · 第二模块·第二周
Clinical Medicine Toolbox
临床医学工具箱
Five focused workshops & interactive days · 五个专题工作坊与互动日

A deep dive into how Western clinical care is practised: the Osler clinical method augmented by AI, surgery pathways and hands-on skills, simulation-based acute care, and problem-based learning through evolving clinical scenarios.

深入了解西方临床医疗实践：融合人工智能的奥斯勒临床方法、外科诊疗路径与动手操作技能、基于模拟的急症救护，以及通过动态临床场景开展的问题导向学习。

  • Day 6: Osler clinical method & AI-assisted diagnosis第六天：奥斯勒临床方法与人工智能辅助诊断
  • Day 7: Surgery overview + suturing skills workshop第七天：外科概述 + 缝合技能工作坊
  • Day 8: Medical simulation — acute care & teamwork第八天：医学模拟 — 急症救护与团队协作
  • Day 9: Problem Based Learning (PBL) session 1 — evolving clinical case第九天：问题导向学习（PBL）第一节 — 动态临床案例
  • Day 10: Problem Based Learning (PBL) session 2 — differential & management第十天：问题导向学习（PBL）第二节 — 鉴别诊断与处理
Block III · Week 3 · 第三模块·第三周
Hospital Observership
医院见习
09:00–13:00 daily · paired shadowing · 每日09:00–13:00·双人跟随见习

Students work in pairs, each assigned to a clinical team for the full week. They observe ward rounds, outpatient clinics, documentation, interprofessional communication, patient safety routines, and real-time clinical decision-making, followed by a daily debrief.

学员两人一组，每组全周跟随一个临床团队。观察查房、门诊、病历记录、跨专业沟通、患者安全规程及实时临床决策过程，每日进行小组汇报。

  • Days 11–15: Daily ward rounds & outpatient observation第11–15天：每日查房与门诊观察
  • Clinical documentation & EMR workflow临床病历记录与电子病历流程
  • Interprofessional team communication跨专业团队沟通
  • Patient safety culture & practices患者安全文化与实践
  • Daily group debrief (reflective learning)每日小组汇报（反思性学习）
Block IV · Week 4 · 第四模块·第四周
TCM–Western Exchange & Madrid
中西医交流与马德里文化体验
Academic exchange + cultural activities · 学术交流 + 文化活动

Two mornings of student-led presentations and moderated debate comparing TCM with Western medicine through shared clinical scenarios. The week closes with three mornings of leisure and cultural activities in Madrid.

两个上午由学生主导演讲，围绕共同临床场景开展中西医对比讨论。课程以三个上午在马德里的休闲文化活动圆满收尾。

  • Day 16: TCM Foundations — student presentations第16天：中医基础 — 学生演讲
  • Day 17: TCM in Modern China + Real clinical cases: TCM meets Western Medicine第17天：中医在现代中国 + 真实案例：中西医结合
  • Days 18–20: Madrid cultural immersion第18–20天：马德里文化体验
